### To center divs:
    <div style="text-align: center">
        <div style="display: inline-block"></div>
    </div>

### Positioning Absolute div inside another div
    Make the outer div 'position: relative' and the inner 'position: absolute'. Thereafter, 'top, bottom, left, right' will be designated to the outer div, and not the entire dom! Outer div's style must also be 'display: inline-block'
